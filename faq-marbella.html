FAQ Marbella

This FAQ provides useful information about Marbella, a coastal town located in the province of Malaga in southern Spain. Marbella is famous for its luxurious lifestyle, beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and world-class golf courses. Visitors can enjoy a Mediterranean climate with mild winters and hot summers, and a variety of outdoor activities such as hiking, water sports, and golf. The best time to visit is during the summer months, and travelers can reach Marbella by flying into Malaga-Costa del Sol Airport. 

What are the best beaches in Marbella?

Marbella is renowned for its beautiful beaches, and there are several stunning options to choose from. Some of the best beaches in Marbella include:

  1. Playa de Nagüeles: A secluded beach nestled between Marbella and Puerto Banús, offering a peaceful escape from the crowds.
  2. Playa de la Fontanilla: Located in the heart of Marbella, this beach boasts golden sands and crystal-clear waters.
  3. Playa del Cable: A long stretch of sand located on the east side of Marbella, popular for its water sports.
  4. Playa de Puerto Banús: A popular beach located in the famous port of Puerto Banús, known for its luxurious atmosphere.
  5. Playa de Rio Real: A long and quiet beach, located near the Rio Real golf course and surrounded by natural sand dunes.

Overall, there is a beach in Marbella to suit every taste, whether you’re looking for lively crowds, water sports, or peaceful relaxation.
