Our all time favorite hotspots and things to do

Welcome to the Choice page of MarbsLifestyle.com! Here, we share our top picks and hidden gems, offering you a gateway to the vibrant lifestyle of Marbella.

Marbella Highlights

Bask under the Andalusian sun at one of Marbella's top 10 beach clubs, where luxury meets leisure. In the city’s heart, discover 25 world-class restaurants serving exquisite cuisine. Begin your day at one of our top 20 breakfast spots, like Rachel's Eco Love or Dezentral, that cater to every palate.

Rest easy at one of Marbella's top six boutique hotels. Immerse yourself in the charm and personalized service of The Marbella Heights or The Town House. If you prefer a homely touch, our top six Bed & Breakfasts, including The Green Village and The Urban Villa, provide a warm and cozy stay.

Indulge in Spain's culinary staple at one of the top six paella restaurants. Whether it's Paella's y Más or Restaurante Valparaiso, a flavorful experience awaits you. Enhance your visit with a round of golf at one of the top 10 golf courses in Marbella, from Los Naranjos to La Quinta.

Exploring Beyond Marbella

Take your culinary journey beyond Marbella to Malaga and Puerto Banus, each boasting 10 exceptional restaurants. Try local dishes at El Pimpi in Malaga or enjoy the opulence at Los Bandidos in Puerto Banus.

Make the most of Marbella's beachside dining with our curated list of 20 lunch spots. Enjoy fresh seafood with a sea view at The Beach House or Victor's Beach.

Marbella After Dark

As the night falls, Marbella turns into a pulsating hotspot. Experience the thrill at the best nightclubs, from the exclusive Olivia Valere to the energetic Pangea. For an unforgettable Sunday night, join the parties at Ocean Club Marbella or Nikki Beach.

Seafood lovers won't be disappointed with our choice of the best seafood restaurants. Try The Sea Grill for a seafood extravaganza, or MC Beach from Marbella Club for a classic hing-end Andalusian culinary experience.

Living in Luxury

Finally, immerse yourself in grandeur at Marbella's best luxury resorts. From the timeless elegance of Marbella Club to the decadence of Puente Romano, these resorts exemplify the best of Marbella's luxury accommodations.

At MarbsLifestyle.com, we aim to make your Marbella experience as unforgettable as the city itself. Explore with us, and create your own Marbella story.
