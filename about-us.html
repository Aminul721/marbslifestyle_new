New in town, Marbslifestyle.com

The location and inspiration guide to Marbella and its beautiful surroundings. Never miss new restaurants and never forget a beach party again. We bring everything together on the unique platform: Marbslifestyle.com

About us

Let's introduce ourselves, we are Simone Loeff and Bart Eichhorn. Founders of Marbslifestyle.com and more than 2 years ago in 2021 started preparing what is now the company Marbslifestyle.com. We have been coming to Marbella several times a year for 10 years and three years ago we bought an apartment in Benahvis.

We are simply in love with Marbella. We love the people, the vibe, the great diversity of hospitality that Marbella has to offer. The parties on Sunday on the beach, but also the small bars that hardly anyone knows. Our ''hidden secrets''.

Marbslifestyle.com is the location and inspiration guide for everything you want to know. From the best restaurants in old town to the most extensive event calendar that Marbella has to offer, so you never miss a thing!

How it all started

Its all about discovering the hidden secrets of Marbella

You probably know it. You keep going to the same beach club, steak restaurant or trendy beach bar. Nice but you want more! You want to get to know the new place. One time you want a luxurious meal, the other time you want entertainment and sometimes just a perfect pasta on the beach.

We and our environment have been asking these questions for years and with Tripadvisor, let's be honest, you just don't get there. Marbella has another demanding public. After doing a lot of research, talking to hundreds of people, we worked for two years on THE GUIDE TO MARBELLA: Marbslifestyle.com. An exclusive location guide with reviews, inspiring content, the most comprehensive event guide and much more.

Get inspired and discover the hidden secrets of Marbella!

What do we offer?

Based on research, a lot of research among thousands of regular and new Marbella visitors, we have created this platform. And, we're not done yet. Many more additional high-quality functionalities will follow in the coming years.

Location guide

Our base is the location guide. All of Marbella's high-end locations in one location. All judged on price and quality. We are continuously expanding this guide. You will only find locations with 4 or 5 stars because we do not settle for less. Are you looking for a unique tapas bar? We found it for you!

The biggest event guide

You don't want to miss anything. The best parties on the beach. That one artist you've always wanted to see comes to Starlite or you're just looking for a nice place to have lunch and you want to make sure there's live music? We bring everything together in the event calendar of Marbella.

Unique events

Unique events and special occasions. From the famous parties at Nikki Beach to dancing in the sand at La Plage Casanis. We have made the most complete overview so that you can easily plan your holiday and make sure you don't miss anything! Is there a new festival or does Mamzel have a new show? We'll be the first to know!
