Filling the gap for the Marbella audience

Bart Eichhorn is CEO and co-founder of Luxury Location Guide, the company behind Marbslifestyle.com. Been coming to Marbella several times a year for 13 years and bought a house 3 years ago. Bart and Simone (co-founder) kept asking themselves, where are we going to eat today? What's new and what events are coming up this weekend. This is how the idea of MarbsLifestyle.com was born. The launch was on May 15, 2023.
